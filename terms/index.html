Harmonexus HMIE is an internal media operations system for Harmonexus. It is used to analyze Harmonexus YouTube content, prepare short-form candidates, manage review workflows, and support approved distribution decisions.

HMIE does not automatically publish to Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, or YouTube without explicit approval through the production workflow.

Platform access is used only for authorized Harmonexus accounts and must comply with the applicable platform terms, API policies, and permission requirements.
