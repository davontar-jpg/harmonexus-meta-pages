Harmonexus HMIE

Privacy Policy

Harmonexus HMIE is used to support Harmonexus media operations, including YouTube-sourced media ingestion, creative review workflows, and approved distribution planning.

HMIE may process account connection status, platform identifiers, publishing metadata, and operational logs needed to connect and validate authorized media workflows. HMIE does not ingest media from Instagram, Facebook, or TikTok.

Data is used only for Harmonexus media operations and platform connection management. Credentials and tokens are stored only in approved secure deployment secret storage and are not published in this repository.

To ask questions about this policy, email harmonexus11@gmail.com.
